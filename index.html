Problem?

We solved this

  • Centralized Member Database
  • Family Grouping
  • Customizable Member Fields
  • Advanced Search & Filtering

about OCIS ghana software

Online Church Information System Ghana, (OCIS GHANA), is a flagship project by Eaglesoft Ghana

Our journey began with a simple but powerful observation: a deep passion for ministry and a commitment to leveraging technology to serve the church. We saw a growing need for a modern, reliable, and easy-to-use system that could help churches of all sizes in Ghana overcome the administrative challenges that often distract from their core mission.

Developed right here in Ghana, our platform is a comprehensive cloud-based solution designed to streamline the operations of churches and religious organizations. We are more than just a software provider; we are partners in ministry, dedicated to empowering church leaders to focus on what truly matters: serving their congregations and spreading the Gospel

At Eaglesoft Ghana, we are committed to providing a secure, intuitive, and locally supported solution that understands the unique needs of the Ghanaian church community. Our system seamlessly integrates key functions—from managing your members to tracking your finances—all within a single platform

Core Features of OCiMS ghana

Our platform is a modular powerhouse, built to handle every aspect of modern church management. Each module is carefully designed to solve a specific problem, providing you with the tools you need to connect your congregation and grow your ministry with confidence.

  • Members

    Managing your congregation shouldn't be a challenge. With our Members module, you can centralize all member data into a single, secure database. Easily add new members, update contact details, and link family members together. Quickly find who you're looking for with a powerful search, and get a clear overview of your church's most valuable asset: its people.

  • Branch Management

    For churches with a vision to expand, this module is essential. Seamlessly manage multiple church branches or campuses from one central platform. Branch Management allows you to oversee administrative tasks, share resources, and synchronize data across all your locations. It ensures consistent standards and provides a unified view of your ministry's growth, no matter how many branches you establish.

  • Assets

    Keep a detailed and organized inventory of all your church's physical assets—from sound equipment and musical instruments to vehicles and property. Our Assets module helps you track every item, ensuring nothing is lost or unaccounted for. You can easily manage maintenance schedules, assign items to departments, and generate reports for a clear picture of your church’s resources

  • Departments & Ministries

    Every great church is built on the efforts of its dedicated departments and ministries. This module gives you the tools to organize, lead, and communicate with your ministry teams. Create and manage departments like Ushers, Choir, or the Youth Ministry, assign leaders, and keep a clear record of team members. It simplifies coordination, empowering your ministry leaders to serve effectively.

  • Events Management

    Coordinating church events, from weekly fellowship meetings to large conferences, is now effortless. Our Events Management module provides all the tools you need to plan, promote, and execute your activities. Create detailed event pages, track attendance, and send targeted communications to attendees. Everything from registration to follow-up is streamlined, ensuring your events are successful and well-attended.

  • communication

    Connecting with your congregation has never been easier. The Communication module provides a powerful suite of tools to ensure your message is always heard. Send personalized mass SMS and email broadcasts to your entire church or use our smart filters to reach specific groups, departments, or ministries. You can also set up automated messages for birthdays, anniversaries, or new member follow-ups, making every member feel valued and engaged. This module saves time and ensures a strong, consistent connection with your entire church body.

  • Finance

    Gain full transparency and control over your church's finances. The Finance module simplifies the management of giving, offerings, and expenses. Easily record and categorize all financial transactions. Most importantly, it enables secure online giving through popular Ghanaian payment platforms, providing your members with a convenient way to support the church. Generate comprehensive financial reports to ensure accountability and effortless planning.
